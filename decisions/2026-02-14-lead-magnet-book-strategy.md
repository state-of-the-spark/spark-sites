# Lead Magnet Book Strategy — Premium Hardbound Authority Book

## Situation

Spark is pivoting from website design/support toward consultancy and best practices. Need a premium lead magnet that:
- Serves as a downsell for prospects who can't afford services
- Positions Grant + Nicole as the go-to consultants in SMB marketing
- Builds personal brand around consultancy
- Is a physical, hardbound book ($20-30) -- not an ebook, not a PDF
- Contains Spark's actual agency solutions
- Feels premium enough to charge $27 and have it feel like a steal

## Decision: Premium Hardbound Book (Hormozi Model)

**Not a lead magnet in the traditional sense.** A real book that IS the authority -- physical, hardbound, something that sits on their desk. Spark's methodology in their hands.

### Positioning

"We work with companies of [X, Y, Z profile]. But if that's not you -- or if you want our best practices first -- get this book."

The book is the downsell. The consultation call is the upsell. Done-for-you services come after.

### Funnel

```
Book ($27 hardcover)
  → Consultation call or group coaching call
    → Done-for-you services
```

Also: Free + shipping campaigns periodically for lead gen bursts.
Also: Bulk copies handed to every prospect who can't afford services.

### What the Book Is NOT

- Not 119 chapters of everything (the current manuscript)
- Not a generic marketing textbook
- Not an ebook or PDF
- Not a quiz or assessment

### What the Book IS

- 12-15 chapters of what actually works, based on 857+ real clients
- Structured around The SPARK Method — a proprietary 5-tier marketing framework
- Matches Spark's experience with their audience's felt pain
- Predicts the future: how SMBs need to adjust NOW for a post-AI world
- Contains real case studies, real numbers, real systems
- Written at a 9th grade reading level, workbook format

## Proposed Structure

### Part 1: The Problem (3-4 chapters)
1. **The Confidence Crisis** — They're spending more and trusting less. 18% confidence stat. You see this every day.
2. **Why Most Marketing Advice Doesn't Work for SMBs** — Built for big budgets, big teams. Not for the owner wearing every hat.
3. **What AI Changed (and What It Didn't)** — The trust paradox. Template sites are dead. Generic content is dead. But human connection, proof, and systems still win.
4. **What We've Seen Across 857 Clients** — Spark's credibility chapter. The patterns that repeat. The mistakes everyone makes.

### Part 2: The Framework (5-7 chapters)
- Each chapter = one component of Spark's proprietary method
- **NAME TBD** — needs to be memorable, ownable, drawable on a napkin
- Should map Spark's 857-client experience onto the demand data
- Must include the Own/Rent/Automate framework for marketing stack
- Should cover the 5-7 things every SMB must build for 2026-2028:
  1. Conversion-optimized website built for AI discovery
  2. First-party data system (email, CRM, customer relationships)
  3. Authentic content engine (human-led, AI-assisted)
  4. Proof system (testimonials, case studies, documented results)
  5. Community or direct audience channel
  6. AI-powered operations layer
  7. Strategic advisor relationship (not tactical vendor)

### Part 3: Implementation (3-4 chapters)
- **First 30 Days** — Refined version of Quick Start Guide
- **Common Mistakes** — From real Spark client experience
- **Case Studies** — 2-3 real Spark clients, before/after with real numbers
- **What's Next** — Clear but non-aggressive CTA. "If you want to do this yourself, you now have everything. If you want help, here's how to start a conversation."

### Appendices
- Self-assessment / marketing scorecard
- Worksheets / templates
- Recommended tools (the 2-3 that matter, not a list of 20)

## CONFIRMED: The Framework Hierarchy (2026-02-14)

Five-tier pyramid. Build from the bottom up. Most SMBs skip to the top and wonder why it doesn't work. Each tier has a "what AI changed" element.

**Framework name: The SPARK Method** (CONFIRMED 2026-02-24)

```
         K — KINDLE the fire
        (Paid ads, 27 impressions)
       ─────────────────────
         R — REACH your market
        (DMs, events, groups)
       ─────────────────────
         A — ATTRACT with content
        (Daily, 10x, value-first)
       ─────────────────────
         P — POSITION your brand
        (You ARE the brand, messaging, website)
       ─────────────────────
         S — STAND for something
        (Marketing is your #1 job)
```

### The SPARK Method — Quick Reference

| Letter | Tier | Meaning | Notes |
|--------|------|---------|-------|
| **S** | Mindset | **S**tand for something | Also: Foundation first. Think like a marketer. Accept that YOU are the marketing department. |
| **P** | Identity | **P**osition your brand | Also: Own your brand. Lead with your brand. You ARE the brand — messaging, website, personal presence. |
| **A** | Content | **A**ttract with content | Also: Attract daily. Release content daily. Value-first, 10x output, creator-led beats AI-generated by 5x. |
| **R** | Outreach | **R**each your market | Also: Move toward people. Connect with people. DMs, events, groups — systematic prospecting, not hoping. |
| **K** | Amplify | **K**indle the fire | Also: Expand with ads. Heat up with ads. Strategic paid amplification — 27 impressions before CTA. Only works if S-P-A-R is built first. |

### Tier Details

| Tier | Felt Pain (from research) | Spark's Solution | AI Layer |
|------|--------------------------|------------------|----------|
| S — STAND | "I'm doing everything myself and drowning" (50% have zero marketing staff) | Accept that YOU are the marketing department | AI makes your messaging CLEARER -- not just faster. AI as a thinking partner for articulating what you actually do. |
| P — POSITION | "I don't know what makes us different" / website as negative selection | Personal brand + messaging + website that doesn't repel | AI commoditized templates -- your HUMAN identity matters more, not less |
| A — ATTRACT | "Content creation is overwhelming" (34% cite this) | Daily content system that doesn't burn you out | AI assists but human-led content wins (creator-led outperforms by 5x) |
| R — REACH | "I need more customers consistently" (60% say #1 challenge) | DMs + events + groups = systematic prospecting | AI can scale outreach but authentic relationships still close deals |
| K — KINDLE | "I'm spending money and can't tell if it's working" (18% confidence) | Strategic awareness ads with 27-impression framework | AI-driven campaigns deliver 22% higher ROI -- but only if foundation is right |

### What Grant Has Seen Work (857 Clients) — Mapped to Tiers

1. **S — STAND**: Accepting that marketing is the top priority of their role
2. **P — POSITION**: Realizing they (and their team) ARE the brand. Stand-out messaging. Website as trust signal (people won't NOT do business because of it).
3. **A — ATTRACT**: Regular daily content. 10x content output. Providing value in groups without selling.
4. **R — REACH**: Direct DMs. Attending niche expos, meetups, live events that target clients attend.
5. **K — KINDLE**: Ongoing awareness ad campaign on Meta and Google (27 impressions before CTA action).
