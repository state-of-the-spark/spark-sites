# Core Messaging Framework

**Parker Street Ministries | Telethon Campaign 2026**
*Based on the Feb 20, 2026 Messaging Session with Sarah Breed, Tim Mitchell, Grant Sparks, Nicole Rhea, and Hannah*

---

## Foundation

**Who PSM serves (telethon audience):** Donors, potential supporters, and the broader Lakeland community — people who want to make a tangible, lasting difference in their own city.

**Who PSM serves (mission):** Families in the Parker Street and North Lakewire neighborhoods of Lakeland, FL.

**Core identity (Sarah Breed):** "Being a good neighbor."

**Core belief (Tim Mitchell):** "Everyone deserves to experience the power and truth of dignity."

**What PSM actually does:** Gospel-centered community development — after-school academics, housing initiatives, family support — focused on just two neighborhoods so the impact goes deep, not wide.

---

## The Template

> "We help [who] [solve what problem] so they can [achieve what outcome]."

---

## Version 1: Formal

*Use on: website hero section, proposals, annual report, LinkedIn, grant cover letters*

> Parker Street Ministries is a gospel-centered community development organization serving the Parker Street and North Lakewire neighborhoods of Lakeland, Florida. For 30 years, we've partnered with families to meet foundational needs — housing, education, and spiritual care — so they have the margin to build stable, thriving lives. We go an inch wide and a mile deep, because lasting change happens through lasting relationships.

**Tagline variant:**
> 30 years of being a good neighbor.

---

## Version 2: Conversational

*Use on: social media captions, casual intros, networking events, email newsletters, telethon host scripts*

> We're Parker Street Ministries — and for 30 years, we've been doing one thing really well: being a good neighbor. We work in just two neighborhoods in Lakeland, helping families find stability so they have room to breathe, to dream, and to build the life they deserve. We're not trying to be everywhere. We'd rather go deep with the people right next door.

**Short social version:**
> 30 years. Two neighborhoods. One mission: be a good neighbor. That's Parker Street Ministries.

---

## Version 3: Elevator Pitch

*Use on: 10-second intros, cold opens, event asks, video hooks — one breath*

> We help families in Lakeland find stability and dignity so they can thrive — not just survive. We've been doing it for 30 years, two neighborhoods deep.

**Even shorter (5 seconds):**
> We're Parker Street. 30 years of being a good neighbor in Lakeland.

## Telethon-Specific Messaging

The telethon has its own audience and energy. These versions layer the campaign on top of the core message.

### Telethon Formal
*Use on: sponsor brochure, press releases, partner outreach*

> The PSM Telethon: Celebrating 30 Years of Parker Street is a milestone virtual event honoring three decades of community impact in Lakeland's Parker Street and North Lakewire neighborhoods. Part fundraiser, part friendraiser, the 90s-themed broadcast invites the community to join in the mission — because being a good neighbor is something everyone can do.

### Telethon Conversational
*Use on: social media, email blasts, host scripts*

> This May, we're throwing the biggest 90s-themed telethon Lakeland has ever seen — and it's for a reason that matters. Parker Street Ministries has been a good neighbor for 30 years. Now it's your turn. Tune in, donate, invite a friend. This isn't just a fundraiser — it's a friendraiser.

### Telethon Hook (one-liner)
> You don't have to live on Parker Street to be a good neighbor.

## Problem → Agitate → Solve (PAS Framework)

*Use when you need to build emotional urgency — ads, video scripts, fundraising asks*

**Problem:**
Right here in Lakeland, hardworking families are one emergency away from losing everything. Not because they're not trying — because the margin isn't there.

**Agitate:**
Imagine working two jobs and still not being able to take your kids on vacation. Imagine feeling like you don't *deserve* a clean home, a stable life, a moment to breathe. That's what cost-burdened families face every day in our own neighborhoods.

**Solve:**
Parker Street Ministries has spent 30 years creating that margin — through housing, education, and care that goes deep, not wide. When families have stability, everything changes. Kids do better in school. Parents breathe. Memories get made. And that's what being a good neighbor looks like.

**Call to Action:**
You can be part of it. Join us at the PSM Telethon this May.

## Audience-Specific Angles

### For Donors / Potential Supporters
- Lead with: **opportunity** ("Be a good neighbor")
- Emphasize: **transparency** (GuideStar, Charity Navigator), **no donor burnout**, **personal thank-yous**
- Feeling: You are part of something lasting, not just writing a check

### For the General Community
- Lead with: **the story** (families, dignity, 30 years)
- Emphasize: **local impact** (this is YOUR city, YOUR neighborhoods)
- Feeling: Pride in Lakeland, connection to something real

### For Partner Organizations / Churches
- Lead with: **collaboration** ("We partner with homeless ministry partners")
- Emphasize: **depth over breadth**, **gospel-centered**, **proven track record**
- Feeling: Alignment with a trusted, transparent ministry
