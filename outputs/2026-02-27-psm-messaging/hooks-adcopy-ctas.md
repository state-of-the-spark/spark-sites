## How to Use This Document

- **Hooks** = opening lines that stop the scroll. Use as video openers, ad headlines, email subject lines, or social post openers.
- **Ad Copy** = full-length caption/script blocks. Ready to pair with creative assets.
- **CTAs** = closing action lines. Swap these into any piece of content.

All copy follows the Language Guide — dignity-first, neighbor language, no grant-speak.

## AD COPY BLOCKS

### Ad 1: The Neighbor Ad (Awareness / Top of Funnel)

**Headline:** You don't have to live on Parker Street to be a good neighbor.

**Body:**
For 30 years, Parker Street Ministries has been doing one thing really well — showing up for families in Lakeland's Parker Street and North Lakewire neighborhoods.

Not with handouts. With housing. With education. With the kind of consistency that lets families breathe.

This May, we're celebrating 30 years at the PSM Telethon — and we're inviting the whole city to be a part of it.

Because being a good neighbor isn't about where you live. It's about what you do.

**CTA:** Join us at the PSM Telethon this May →

### Ad 2: The Margin Ad (Emotional / Mid-Funnel)

**Headline:** She went on vacation for the first time.

**Body:**
A family in our housing program saved enough to take their kids on a trip. First time ever.

That's not a statistic. That's what margin looks like.

When hardworking families have stable housing, after-school support, and a community that knows their name — they don't just survive. They thrive.

Parker Street Ministries has been creating that margin for 30 years. Two neighborhoods. Thousands of families. Deep, lasting relationships.

**CTA:** Help create more margin → Support the PSM Telethon

### Ad 3: The Friendraiser Ad (Telethon-Specific)

**Headline:** Part fundraiser. Part friendraiser. All 90s.

The PSM Telethon: Celebrating 30 Years of Parker Street is coming this May — and it's unlike any nonprofit event you've been to.

Think 90s theme. Think real stories from real neighbors. Think a community coming together because they actually want to, not because they feel guilted into it.

We're raising $500,000 to sustain the mission. But we're also raising something harder to measure — awareness, connection, and pride in what Lakeland can do when we show up for each other.

**CTA:** Save the date. Invite a friend. Be a good neighbor →

### Ad 4: The Trust Ad (Donor Conversion / Bottom of Funnel)

**Headline:** We don't burn out our donors. We build relationships.

Parker Street Ministries has the highest transparency ratings from GuideStar and Charity Navigator. We send hand-signed thank you letters. And we've been doing this for 30 years — in the same two neighborhoods — because we believe in going deep, not wide.

Your support goes directly to housing, education, and family stability for neighbors right here in Lakeland.

### Ad 5: The Dignity Ad (Brand / Evergreen)
