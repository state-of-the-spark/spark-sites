### The Mature Tree Preservation Model (MTPM)
This is Wyatt's biggest differentiator. A structured qualitative assessment framework that evaluates six interrelated preservation domains to determine whether long-term tree retention is biologically sustainable and legally defensible.

**The Six Domains:**
1. Physiological Capacity — crown vitality, decay progression, compartmentalization response
2. Structural Architecture — codominant unions, attachment integrity, load path continuity
3. Root-Zone Integrity — soil compaction, CRZ disturbance, infrastructure conflicts
4. Environmental & Anthropogenic Stressors — construction proximity, irrigation shifts, wind exposure
5. Intervention Feasibility — reduction thresholds, pruning limits, diminishing returns
6. Projected Service Life — longevity horizon, risk trajectory, cost-benefit analysis

**Three-Tier Classification:**
- Favorable — preservation supported
- Conditional — preservation with stipulations
- Limited — preservation not supported

**Why It Matters:**
- Legally defensible (structured methodology holds up in court and deposition)
- Unique in the industry (no competitor has a named, structured framework)
- Decision-enabling (gives tree owners and developers a clear, documented determination)
- Marketable (positions Wyatt as the authority, not just an opinion)

**Court-Ready Language (from Wyatt's own documentation):**
"This evaluation was conducted utilizing the Mature Tree Preservation Model (MTPM), a structured qualitative assessment framework examining physiological capacity, structural architecture, root-zone integrity, environmental stressors, intervention feasibility, and projected service life. Each domain is classified into a tiered viability category to determine the defensibility of long-term retention."

This model should be the centerpiece of every piece of marketing. It is the reason attorneys hire him. It is the reason developers should hire him. It is the reason management companies need him on retainer.

## Service Categories

### 1. Embedded Consulting Arborist (Retainer)
On-call technical authority for tree companies and management firms. Wyatt serves as their third-party expert — not competing with their crews, but making their work defensible.

**What's Included:**
- On-call arborist consultations
- On-site production observation and crew mentorship
- Tree risk assessments and removal justification
- Client-facing consultation appointments (representing the company)
- Written documentation (advisory memos, risk opinions, removal justifications)
- Safety program implementation and crew training
- Administrative and business procedure guidance
- The MTPM applied to their portfolio

### 2. Preservation Strategy Briefs (Project-Based)
Wyatt's signature deliverable. A structured written report applying the MTPM to high-value trees on estate properties, development sites, HOA disputes, or pre-construction assessments.

**Brief Structure:**
- Cover page with property details and credentials
- Executive summary with MTPM classification and final determination
- Full six-domain assessment with tier classification and narrative justification
- Preservation determination (Supported / Conditional / Not Supported)
- Recommended preservation plan with monitoring intervals
- Professional limitation statement

**Use Cases:**
- Estate properties with mature live oaks
- HOA disputes over tree removal
- Pre-construction site assessments for developers
- Removal vs. retention conflicts
- Insurance and litigation support

### 3. Expert Witness & Litigation Support
Expert testimony in tree-related litigation. Property disputes, insurance claims, development conflicts, standard-of-care evaluations, tree failure analysis.

### 4. On-Site Intensives (One-Time)
Short-duration high-value engagements for production observation, crew mentorship, and risk documentation.
